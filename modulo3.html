English Module

Module Description

The objective of this module is to develop oral, listening and reading communication skills in English. that facilitate interaction with English-speaking people over a wide range of issues related to the maintenance of information systems.

This knowledge is important since the business areas do not have the flexibility to adapt to the changes that requires the international environment, because they do not have an area that provide maintenance of computer systems in English, involving loss of business opportunities, growth, market presence and to non-compliance with legal regulations.

Vocabulary

Plug something in

Connect something to the socket.

Switch something on/off

Turn something on or off by pressing a button or switch.

Log in/on

To perform the actions that allow you to begin using a computer system.

Log off/out

To perform the actions that allow you to finish using a computer system.

Click on

To carry on a computer operation by pressing a button on the mouse or keyboard

Personal Learning

Thanks to this module I have enriched my vocabulary, now I can read documents about my specialty that are in English and understand their content. Not only has my technical English improved, it has also improved my level of English in general.
